2025. MineXR. Building Open XR Mines for Safe Enhanced Mining Education

An innovative teaching tool for mining engineering to: assist the learning process of students; offer potential to teachers to receive support in the preparation of theoretical content; promote learners’ self-determination, unique learning experiences, and to support new approaches to teaching and developing cognitive skills; engage in Collaborative Online International Learning courses (COILs). Partners: UPC (Coordination), Wroclaw University (Poland), Polito (Italy), TU Graz (Austria), and Khmelnytskyi National University (Ukraine).
